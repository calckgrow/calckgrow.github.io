Fun Facts About Age

  • In many East Asian cultures, you are considered "1 year old" on the day you are born.
  • The oldest verified person ever lived to be 122 years and 164 days old.
  • Your age on other planets is different! (e.g., A 30-year-old on Earth is only 15 on Mars).

Frequently Asked Questions

How is age calculated legally?

In most countries, age increases by one year on your birth date. However, in legal terms (like turning 18), the exact time of birth usually doesn't matter, just the date.
