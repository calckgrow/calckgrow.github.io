Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are these estimates?
While no third-party tool can be 100% precise, CalcGrow uses a sophisticated algorithm that considers niche RPM (Revenue Per Mille), location, and engagement. We provide a range (Min-Max) to account for ad-blockers and demonetized views.
Does this tool work for Shorts?
Yes! Select the "Shorts / Memes" category in the Niche dropdown. Shorts RPM is significantly lower ($0.01 - $0.06) compared to long-form videos.
How is the "Brand Deal" estimated?
Brand deal estimates are calculated based on your subscriber count and average view count, using industry-standard CPV (Cost Per View) rates for sponsored integrations.
